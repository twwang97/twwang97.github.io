Tsung-Wun Wang (王琮文) received a B.S. degree in Mechanical Engineering from National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2020. His research interests include control theory, robotics, and computer vision. He is applying for admission to a PhD degree specializing in control systems, with the ultimate goal of becoming a professor and having the opportunity to teach and do research at a university.

 
 
 
 

Experience

Tsung-Wun Wang has extensive research and professional experience from controls and robotics to computer vision and mechanism design. His projects include adaptive control design , wheeled inverted pendulum , robotic arms etc. The most exciting event in 2019 was his participation in HIWIN robot competition and his team won NT$ 10,0000 in total.
